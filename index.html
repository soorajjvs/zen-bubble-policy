This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Advertising (AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • App usage data
  • Analytics data related to ads
  • General device information (model, OS version, etc.)

This data is collected by Google only for ad personalisation, analytics, and fraud prevention. **We do not access or store this information ourselves.**

b. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect:

  • Email
  • Photos
  • Contacts
  • Location
  • Payment info

No personal data is saved or sent to our servers.
